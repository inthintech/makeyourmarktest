Make Your Mark

The Innovative Solution Built for Analytics and Reporting of your Examination Results.

What is Make Your Mark

Make Your Mark is a cloud based web analytics tool designed specifically for Educational Institutions. We help you to make informed decisions based on the marks scored by your students in examinations.

Why do you need it

Make Your Mark is the simple solution to complex administration questions traditionally solved by huge time consuming process if any available. Simply put, you can get answers to questions like

What is the Pass Percentage of institution in the last examination?

Which is the top performing class of my department?

Which subject has the least no of students passed in my institution?

And more..

Technology and User Experience

Make Your Mark has been created with state of the art web technologies delivering a simple to use modern application for the digital age. Data security is a priority for us and we understand your demand.

Uploading your results in our system is relatively very simple and we allow you to create your own internal users for your institution.

Data Visualizations

Not only providing good clean reports, we also provide you data visualization charts helping you to understand the overall in one picture.
